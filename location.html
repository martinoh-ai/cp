Getting Here

Masia Can Pares is nestled in the peaceful hills of Sant Pere de Ribes, offering the perfect escape while remaining easily accessible from Barcelona and the coast.

The property is best reached by car, with ample free parking on-site. The scenic drive from Barcelona takes you through beautiful Catalan countryside.
