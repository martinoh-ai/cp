Experiences

From sunrise yoga in the botanical gardens to sunset padel matches, discover a world of activities designed to nourish body and soul.

Impossible to get bored—unless you choose to.

Can Pares is more than a place to stay. It's a base for discovery. The surrounding region offers world-class beaches, historic towns, renowned wineries, and trails through Mediterranean nature.

Whether you seek adventure or relaxation, culture or nature, you'll find it within easy reach of our gates.

Your House Guide As soon as you book, you'll receive the house guide: a curated list of awesome things to do in the house and to discover in the region—our personal recommendations for an unforgettable stay.

Tourism

Discover the Region

Sitges · 10 min

Sitges Beaches & Old Town

17 beaches, a charming old town, the Cau Ferrat Museum with El Greco paintings, and vibrant nightlife. The perfect Mediterranean escape just minutes away.

Penedès · 20 min

Wine Country & Cava

Spain's premier cava region. Visit historic bodegas like Codorníu and Freixenet, taste world-class wines, and enjoy vineyard lunches with stunning views.

Barcelona · 35 min

Barcelona

Gaudí's masterpieces, world-class museums, incredible food scene, and the vibrant energy of one of Europe's great cities—all within easy reach.
