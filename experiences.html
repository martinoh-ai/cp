A Hidden Paradise

Your Private Botanical Sanctuary

Both guest houses share an extraordinary botanical garden spanning over 20,000m², home to more than 600 different trees and countless hidden corners waiting to be discovered.

An 18-meter swimming pool sits serenely within this green paradise, offering the perfect spot for morning laps or afternoon relaxation. This is a confidential retreat, hidden from the world, where nature embraces you at every turn.

Every path leads to a new discovery—a shaded reading nook, a sun-drenched terrace, a fragrant herb garden. This is your private Mediterranean escape.
